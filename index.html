Our Jobs

In 2019, a total of 50bred black cattle were brought from the state of Kazakhstan by the head and founder of the enterprise. In the same year, the head of the enterprise brought various types of feed from Ukraine in order to feed the black cattle properly, fatten them up quickly and grow up with the right diet, and to achieve the desired result. To date, the number of livestock brought to the enterprise has reached 500 , and 2,700 liters of dairy products and 30,000 kg of meat products are delivered to processing enterprises.

On 100 hectares of 400 hectares land belonging to the enterprise, intensive gardens planted by drip irrigation based on Chinese technology have been established. The advantage of intensive orchards is that it is easy to work with them because the trunks of the trees are small, and the quality of the fruit is high because of the light and air circulation in the orchards. It also has many conveniences in picking its fruit.

I Livestock fodder crops and other types of agricultural crops are planted on 300 hectares of land. In addition, the company is supplied with breeding livestock, poultry, milk and apple seedlings as a supplier of preferential credits given to our people by the state.
